Mais Con Yelo

The perfect dish to refresh yourself on a hot and humid day.

Image and recipe from The Unlikely Baker

Ingredients

1 12-oz can canned corn whole kernel or peaches and cream
1 13-oz can creamed corn
condensed milk to taste
ice shaved or crushed
evaporated milk to taste
cornflakes optional
vanilla ice cream optional

Instructions

  1. In a glass (or any serving container), layer your ingredients, to taste. For a 12-oz glass, I usually do: 2 tablespoons whole corn kernels, 2 tablespoons creamed corn and 1-2 teaspoons condensed milk.
  2. Fill the glass with shaved or crushed ice.
  3. Pour evaporated milk. The amount depends on the size of the glass you're using. Ensure your ice is mostly wet but not soaked.
  4. Optional: top with ice cream and cornflakes.
  5. Mix and enjoy!
