About Pagkain ng Pinas

Mabuhay! Welcome to Pagkain ng Pinas, the best place to look for Filipino dishes for any time of day.


What is our mission?

Our mission is to celebrate and share the colorful Filipino culture to everyone by offering splendid and easy-to-follow recipes.

What do we offer?

  • Beloved Filipino recipes that are fun to do and easy to follow.
  • A chance to get your own recipe featured on our page.

What can we find from our website?

Home Page: We warmly welcome you with a glimpse of our favorite dishes from each category we offer.
Categories Page: We offer 4 categories – breakfast, lunch, dinner, and dessert. Below the card of each category, you will find tasty recipes that you can simply follow by clicking the “View Recipe” button. Here, we give you a brief description of the dish, the ingredients needed, and step-by-step instructions.
About Us Page: This is the page you are in right now! We give an overview and an introduction of the cite of Pagkain ng Pinas.
Submit Recipe Page: Want to share a Filipino recipe with us? Fill out the form and submit it to us through this page.

What is our social media?

@pagkain_ng_pinas on Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and X (Twitter).
